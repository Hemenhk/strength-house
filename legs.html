Legs

Squats

5 sets of 5 reps

1. Place the barbell on your upper back, so that it rests right below the traps.
2. Stand slightly wider than shoulder width, and place your feet paralleled to each other, and toes pointed slightly outwards.
3. Slowly squat down to when your knees are bent just below 90 degrees.
4. Push with your legs until you are standing upright. Repeat.

Squat Video

Leg Extensions

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Seated on the machine, extend your legs upwards and squeeze the muscles for a much stronger contraction.
2. Slowly bring your legs down again and stop before they have lost activation. Repeat.

Leg Curls

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Whilst seated, curl your legs downwards and squeeze the muscles for a strong contraction.
2. Slowly bring your legs up again and stop before they have lost activation. Repeat.

Lunges

4 sets of 10 reps

1. With a barbell on your back, take slow lunges forwards for 10 steps (5 for each leg).

Calf Raises

3 sets of as many reps as possible

1. Stand with the balls of your feet on a rack, placed right underneath the bar of a smith machine.
2) Raise your calves as high as you can and really squeeze at the top for a very strong contraction.
3) Slowly bring your calves down again. Repeat.

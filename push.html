Tackle The Chest, Triceps and Shoulders

Chest

Bench Press

5 sets of 5 reps

1. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder width. Sligtly arch your back and press the bar away from you.
2. Balance the bar whilst bringing it down to your chest.
3. Press the bar upwards and repeat.

Bench Press Video

Incline Dumbell Press

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Balance both arms, and bring your elbows slightly below 180 degrees, so that they are at an angle.
2. Press the dumbells upwards and bring them slightly together at the top for a better contraction.
3. Bring the elbows down to a 90 degree angle. Further and you risk hurting your front delts.

High Cable Flyes

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Set the cables to be higher than your shoulders.
2. Grab the handles and bring your hands to meet at the lower portion of your pecs. Squeeze for a better contraction.
3. Slowly bring the handles back, but stop where your elbows are parallelled to your back, then repeat.

Low Cable Flyes

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Set the cables to be below your knees.
2. Grab the handles and bring your hands to meet right below your chin. Squeeze for a better contraction.
3. Slowly bring the handles back down, but stop right before they come to a full stop, so as to keep the muscles in action.

Triceps

Triceps Rope Extensions

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Grab the rope and take a couple of steps back, arch your back a bit.
2. Push the rope towards your hips and extend the hands to either side of the hips, squeeze for a better contraction.
3. Slowly bring the rope back up, whilst keeping the elbows in a fixed position. Repeat

Triceps Pushdown

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Grab the bar and take a couple of steps back, arch your back a bit.
2. Push the bar towards your hips and lock out your elbows for a stronger contraction.
3. Slowly bring the bar back up, whilst keeping the elbows in a fixed position. Repeat.

Skullcrushers

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Grab the barbell and hold it shoulder width.
2. Lay down on the bench with your head right by the edge.
3. Push the bar up like in a bench press.
4. Bring the barbell up beyond your forehead and lock your elbows in place.
5.With elbows locked, bring the barbell down beyond your head and press up. Repeat.

Shoulders

Dumbbell Shoulder Press

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Seated on a bench, grab dumbells and press them over your head.
2. Bring the dumbbells down, and stop when your elbows reach 90 degrees.
3. Going beyond 90 degrees will lead to injury.

Lateral Raises

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Standing upright, grab two dumbbells.
2. Hold the dumbbells on the side of your hips, and raise them up to shoulder height.
3. Slowly bring the dumbbells down and stop just before you loose the activation. Repeat.

Rope Face Pulls

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Set the rope to be on the same level as your forehead.
2. Pull the rope towards your face and extend your elbows to either side in a 90 degree angle.
3. Keep your thumbs pointed upwards to better contract the rear deltoid, so as to remove the traps from the movement.
Slowly bring the rope back and stop just before your loose the connection. Repeat.
